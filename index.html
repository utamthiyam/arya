Aryabhata (ISO: Āryabhaṭa) or Aryabhata (476–550 CE)[3][6] was an Indian mathematician and astronomer of the classical age of Indian mathematics and Indian astronomy. He flourished in the Gupta Era and produced works such as the Aryabhatiya (which mentions that in 3600 Kali Yuga, 499 CE, he was 23 years old)and the Arya-siddhanta.
